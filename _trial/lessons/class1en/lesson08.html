Lesson 1.8: More of to be

Vocabulary

▷ boring
▷ complicated
▷ dangerous
▷ easy
▷ hard
▷ impossible
▷ interesting
▷ polite
▷ possible
▷ rude
▷ safe
▷ simple

Explanation

1. It’s easy to learn English.

▶
C1L08-Explanation1.mp3
  1. ▷ It’s hard to learn Japanese.
  2. ▷ It’s necessary to do a good job.
  3. ▷ It isn’t good to be lazy.
  4. ▷ It is wrong to go that way.
  5. ▷ It’s important to have friends.
  6. ▷ It’s good for you to get up early in the morning.
  7. ▷ It’s bad to smoke cigarettes.
  8. ▷ It’s not easy to answer that question.
  9. ▷ It’s amazing to travel around the world.
  10. ▷ It’s terrible to stay up late at night.
  11. ▷ It’s hard to believe they could think about such a thing.
  12. ▷ It’s impossible to do it.
  13. ▷ It’s easy for him to answer the question.
  14. ▷ It’s smart of him to answer the question.

2: Verb + ing => it

▶
C1L08-Explanation2.mp3
  1. ▷ Learning how to cook is easy.
  2. ▷ Saving money for the future is necessary.
  3. ▷ Listening to music is fun.
  4. ▷ Loving you is easy because you are beautiful.
  5. ▷ Staying up late makes me feel tired.
  6. ▷ Talking to him helps me understand the problem.
  7. ▷ Breaking up with him is the right thing to do.
  8. ▷ Talking to her is interesting.
  9. ▷ Crying doesn’t help solve the problem.

3: So and Such

▶
C1L08-Explanation3.mp3
  1. ▷ That movie is so good. / It’s such a good movie.
  2. ▷ The idea is so bad. / This is such a bad idea.
  3. ▷ The teacher is so patient with his students. / He’s such a patient teacher with his students.
  4. ▷ You are so good. / You are such a good friend.
  5. ▷ The crocodile is so big. / It’s such a big crocodile.
  6. ▷ The baby is so cute. / He’s such a cute baby.

4: There is / There are

▶
C1L08-Explanation4.mp3
  1. ▷ There is a book on the chair.
  2. ▷ There are some cats on the grass.
  3. ▷ There is a man on the phone.
  4. ▷ There are four weeks in a month.
  5. ▷ There is a blue car in the garage.
  6. ▷ There are ten books in a box.
  7. ▷ Is there a museum in your hometown?
  8. ▷ Are there any birds in the garden?
