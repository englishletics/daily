  • ▷ Who is she?
  • ▷ She is my mother.
  1. ▷ Who is he?
  2. ▷ He is my mechanic.
  3. ▷ Who is she?
  4. ▷ She is my mother.
  5. ▷ Who is a football player?
  6. ▷ The man next to the door is a football player.
  7. ▷ Who is a priest?
  8. ▷ The man between me and my friend is a priest.
  9. ▷ Who is your favorite singer?
  10. ▷ Taylor Swift is my favorite singer.
  11. ▷ Who is your favorite actress?
  12. ▷ My Tam is my favorite actress.
  13. ▷ Who is the richest man in the world?
  14. ▷ Bill Gates is the richest man in the world.
  15. ▷ Who is the smartest student in the class?
  16. ▷ My son is the smartest student in the class.
  17. ▷ Who is the most important person in the company?
  18. ▷ Ms. Smith is the most important person in the company.
  19. ▷ Who is the most famous actor in the world?
  20. ▷ Brad Pitt is the most famous actor in the world.
  21. ▷ Who is under the table?

  23. ▷ Who is next to Mr. Jones?
