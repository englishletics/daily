Dialogue

▶
C1L04-Native-Dialogue.mp3

▷ Do you love to listen to music? Yes, I love to listen to music.

▷ Do you like to hang out with friends?
Yes, I like to hang out with friends.

▷ Do you try to learn English? No, I don’t try to learn English. I try to learn Japanese.
▷ Do you want to travel to Korea? No, I don’t want to travel to Korea. I want to travel to Malaysia.

▷ Do you need to take a selfie? No, I don’t need to take a selfie. I need to take a photo.

▷ Do you have to call your mother? No, I don’t have to call my mother. I have to call my sister.

▷ Do you hate to do homework? No, I don’t hate to do homework. I hate to do housework.

▷ Do you intend to talk to the doctor? Yes, I intend to talk to the doctor and I also intend to talk to the nurse.

▷ Do you expect to have a baby next year? No, I don’t expect to have a baby next year. I expect to have a baby in three years.

▷ Do you pretend to like her? Yes, I just pretend to like her.
