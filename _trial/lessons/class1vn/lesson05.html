▶ 1. Building sentences with one verb and verb + 'to' + verb

  1. ▷ Kim sees snakes. / Kim hates to see snakes.
    Kim thấy rắn / Kim ghét thấy rắn.

  2. ▷ The secretary studies English. / The secretary tries to study English.
