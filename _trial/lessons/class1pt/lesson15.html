I. The verb “To be”

▷ he / she / it was
▷ I was
▷ they were
▷ we were
▷ you were
▷ you were
  1. ▷ I was tired last night. Were you?
    Yes, I was tired last night.
    Eu estava cansado ontem à noite. Você estava também?
    Sim, estava cansado ontem à noite.
  2. ▷ Were you bored without me yesterday?
    No, I wasn’t bored without you yesterday.
    Você estava entediado sem mim ontem?
    Não, eu não estava entediado sem você ontem.
  3. ▷ Was she at home last week?
    No, she was not at home last week. Ela estava em casa na semana passada?
    Não, ela não estava em casa na semana passada.
  4. ▷ Jane didn’t come to class yesterday. Was she sick?
    Yes, she was sick.
    Jane não veio à aula ontem. Ela estava doente?
    Sim, estava doente.

II. Action Verbs

  1. ▷ Did you sister help you do homework? No, My sister didn’t help me do anything. A sua irmã ajudou você a fazer o dever de casa?
    Não, minha irmã não me ajudou a fazer nada.

  2. ▷ Did Sarah finish her homework? Yes, Sarah finished it. Sarah terminou fazer seu dever de casa?
    Sim, Sarah terminou.

  3. ▷ Did he love her? Yes, he loved her but he didn’t want to marry her. Ele a amava?
    Sim, ele a amava, mas ele não queria se casar com ela.
  4. ▷ Did James come to class yesterday? Yes, he came to class yesterday. James veio à aula ontem?
    Sim, ele veio à aula ontem.
